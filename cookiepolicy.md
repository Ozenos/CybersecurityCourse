# Cookie Policy

**Last updated:** 18/12/2025

This Cookie Policy explains how our booking web application (“the Service”) uses cookies and similar technologies.

This policy should be read together with our Privacy Policy.

---

## 1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites function properly, improve security, and enhance user experience.

Cookies can be:
- **Session cookies** (deleted when you close your browser)
- **Persistent cookies** (remain on your device for a defined period)

---

## 2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies **only where strictly necessary** for the operation of the Service.

We do **not** use cookies for:
- Advertising
- Tracking across websites
- Analytics or profiling
- Marketing purposes

---

## 3. Types of Cookies We Use

### 3.1 Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are essential for the Service to function and cannot be disabled without impacting core functionality.

They are used for:
- User authentication
- Session management
- Security (e.g. preventing unauthorized access)

**Legal basis:**  
These cookies are processed under **legitimate interests** and **performance of a contract** (Article 6(1)(b) and 6(1)(f) GDPR).  
User consent is **not required** for strictly necessary cookies under EU law.

---

## 4. Third-Party Cookies

We do not use third-party cookies for advertising or analytics.

If third-party services are used for hosting or infrastructure purposes, they may set cookies strictly necessary for security or service delivery. Such cookies are subject to the respective providers’ policies.

---

## 5. Cookie Duration

- Session cookies expire when you close your browser.
- Persistent cookies, if used, are stored only for the duration necessary to fulfill their purpose.

---

## 6. Managing Cookies

Most web browsers allow you to control or delete cookies through their settings.

Please note that disabling strictly necessary cookies may prevent you from:
- Logging into your account
- Making or managing reservations
- Using authenticated features of the Service
