We collect only the personal data necessary to provide and operate the Service.

## 2. Legal Basis and Purpose of Processing

We process your personal data under the following legal bases as defined by Article 6 of the GDPR:

- **Performance of a contract (Article 6(1)(b))**
  - Creating and managing user accounts
  - Allowing users to make and manage reservations

- **Legitimate interests (Article 6(1)(f))**
  - Displaying reservation information to authenticated users
  - Ensuring platform security and preventing misuse

- **Legal obligations (Article 6(1)(c))**
  - Compliance with applicable laws and regulations

## 3. Visibility of Reservations

Reservation details are displayed on the main page of the Service.

The identity of the reserver (email address) is visible **only to authenticated users** and only to the extent necessary for the proper use of the Service, in accordance with the principles of data minimization and purpose limitation.

Visitors who are not logged in cannot see the identity of reservers.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data.

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect personal data, including:

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this Privacy Policy.
